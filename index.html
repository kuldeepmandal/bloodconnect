About BloodConnect

Our Mission

BloodConnect is a web-based system designed to bridge the gap between blood donors and recipients. Our mission is to create a seamless, efficient, and user-friendly platform that facilitates urgent blood requests and empowers individuals to take action. We aim to build a strong community of donors and ensure that no one has to face a blood shortage alone. By leveraging technology, we connect people when it matters most, all while promoting and encouraging regular blood donation as a key part of public health.

Key Features

  • Easy Registration: Simple and quick process for donors to join the community.
  • Real-Time Requests: Post urgent blood requests with details like blood type, hospital, and urgency level.
  • Smart Search: Efficiently find eligible donors in your city, filtered by blood type and donation history.
  • User Dashboard: A personalized dashboard to manage your profile and view pending blood requests.
  • Data Visualization: View a graphical representation of blood type distribution among available donors on your dashboard.
